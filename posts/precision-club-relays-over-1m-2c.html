Precision Club Relays over 1M - 2♣

This is a simple relay in Precision club after 1M - 2♣, which is artificial and game forcing. The major opener shows their entire shape, followed by controls then honor scans. Responder generally bids the next step to allow the opener to continue to describe their hand. Further details can be found in various Kit's Korner posts, such as The Death Response. This set of relays is intended to be easy to remember.
